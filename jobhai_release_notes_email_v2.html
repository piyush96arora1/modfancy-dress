Release Details
RELEASE 1 Candidate Quality Responses + In-Chat Job CTAs 8 Mar 2026
What Changed
  • Structured responses for candidate quality complaints — branches by sub-issue (no applicants, wrong profiles, no response, can't see applicants)
  • Bot acknowledges frustration before giving resolution steps
  • Relax Job Requirements CTA — inline action to edit job criteria from chat
  • Boost Job CTA — surfaces contextually for low-response jobs
  • Bot references real account data: application counts, unlock usage
  • Removed helpline deflection for candidate quality queries
Measured Impact
  • Deflection: 23.4% → 8.3%  (↓ 65%)
  • Key driver of overall deflection drop 14.5% → 6.6%
  • 5 recruiters clicked job improvement CTAs (2 Relax, 3 Boost)
  • First personalised account-aware bot responses
Before

"I understand you're not getting candidates. Please contact our customer support team."

After

"I can see your job has 12 applications. Since candidates aren't responding — [Relax Job Requirements] [Boost Job] or use your 60 free unlocks to reach out directly."

RELEASE 2 Credit Query Handling 8 Mar 2026
What Changed
  • Bot reads live credit balance, transaction history, and expiry data from platform API
  • Per-order expiry breakdown shown inline (e.g. '210 credits expiring Jul 03, 2026')
  • Buy Credits in-chat redirect action added
  • 'Why are my credits 0' queries resolved by fetching actual transaction history
Measured Impact
  • Deflection: 10.1% → 6.7%
  • First live data integration — real credit status, not generic guidance
  • Credit expiry table UI deployed inline in chat
  • Eliminates 'credits are 0' frustrated return visits
Before

"Credits may have expired. Please contact support to check your balance."

After

"Your balance is 0. No transactions in the last 30 days. [Per-order expiry table] [Buy Credits]"

RELEASE 3 Invoice & Billing Self-Serve 13 Mar 2026
What Changed
  • Bot handles invoice queries end-to-end — no longer deflects to billing team
  • Explains invoice is sent to registered email
  • Step-by-step guide: Profile → Transaction History → Request Invoice
  • GST invoice queries handled inline
Measured Impact
  • Deflection: 100% (every query, Feb 19–Mar 11) → 0% (Mar 13+)
  • Longest-standing gap since chatbot launch — now closed
  • Full category removed from human support queue
Before

"For invoice requests, please contact the billing team directly." (100% of the time)

After

"Invoice sent to your email. To request manually: Profile → Transaction History → Request Invoice."
